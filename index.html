During this period we also started testing how we would bend pipes to fit in the cubesat, while others began designing the layout of the pipes in the cubesat.

However, we changed directions from fluid flow to a stationary fluid, and with this, our design shifted towards using manifolds instead (although no one is quite sure on what a manifold is)
